# ReputationDAO

ReputationDAO is a revolutionary governance protocol that transforms traditional DAO decision-making through liquid reputation-weighted voting systems. The protocol introduces Contextual Governance Domains where different types of decisions require specific expertise, with technical proposals weighted toward verified developers, treasury decisions favoring financial management experts, and community initiatives prioritizing proven social coordinators. Governance power dynamically shifts based on demonstrated expertise and measurable contribution history through innovative Temporal Reputation Mining mechanisms.

The technical architecture implements sophisticated smart contracts with commitment schemes requiring reputation token staking during governance participation, coupled with rewards for successful proposals and penalties for consistently backing failed initiatives. The system features advanced Reputation Oracles integrating external verification sources, Anonymous Expertise Voting using zero-knowledge proofs, and Cross-Domain Reputation Transfer mechanisms with natural decay through Reputation Half-Life mechanics. Core blockchain features include liquid delegation with time-locks, verifiable reputation minting workflows, and Expertise Verification Challenges enabling community-driven peer review processes.

This protocol enables fluid governance structures that adapt to member availability while maintaining quality control through Reputation-Backed Proposal Bonds and natural spam prevention. Key applications include decentralized protocol governance requiring developer consensus for technical changes, investment DAO management with financial expert weighting, and community organization governance guided by proven builders. The system creates sustainable governance ecosystems where expertise mobility and verifiable competency drive more informed decision-making across diverse organizational contexts.
